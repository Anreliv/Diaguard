Diaguard has now two distinct themes: Light, as we know it, and the all-new, more eye-friendly Dark.

The theme may be changed at any time via the app settings and may adjust itself to the current time of day. The system-based night mode, introduced with Android 9, is supported as well.

Additional improvements
  • Added option to filter branded food through the app settings

  • Deleted food will not come back

  • Fixed duplicate search results

  • Optimizations for small displays
